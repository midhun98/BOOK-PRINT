Quantitative Aptitude - Solved Paper (Time, Distance and Work)

1. A train travels at an average speed of 50 km/h for 30 minutes, then increases to 70 km/h for the next 45 minutes. What is the average speed of the train for the entire journey?
A) 60 km/h
B) 62 km/h
C) 58 km/h
D) 65 km/h
2. If a person can complete a job in 10 days and another person can complete the same job in 15 days, how many days will it take for both persons working together to complete the job?
A) 5 days
B) 6 days
C) 12 days
D) 4 days
3. Two trains are moving in opposite directions at speeds of 60 km/h and 90 km/h respectively. If the length of each train is 250 meters, how long will it take for them to completely pass each other?
A) 12 seconds
B) 15 seconds
C) 18 seconds
D) 10 seconds
4. A pipe can fill a tank in 6 hours. Due to a leak in the tank, it takes 8 hours to fill the tank. If the tank is full, how much time will it take for the leak to empty the tank?
A) 24 hours
B) 48 hours
C) 36 hours
D) 30 hours
5. A man covers a certain distance at 10 km/h and returns at 15 km/h. Find his average speed for the entire journey.
A) 12 km/h
B) 12.5 km/h
C) 11.5 km/h
D) 12.25 km/h
6. If 5 men or 7 women can complete a piece of work in 37 days, how many days will it take for 7 men and 6 women working together to complete the same work?
A) 10 days
B) 12 days
C) 15 days
D) 9 days

Answer Key and Explanations

1. Answer: B) 62 km/h

The train travels at 50 km/h for 30 minutes (0.5 hours) and 70 km/h for 45 minutes (0.75 hours).
Distance covered in first part = 50 × 0.5 = 25 km
Distance covered in second part = 70 × 0.75 = 52.5 km
Total distance = 25 + 52.5 = 77.5 km
Total time = 0.5 + 0.75 = 1.25 hours
Average speed = Total distance / Total time = 77.5 / 1.25 = 62 km/h

2. Answer: B) 6 days

First person's work rate = 1/10 job per day
Second person's work rate = 1/15 job per day
Combined work rate = 1/10 + 1/15 = (3+2)/30 = 5/30 = 1/6 job per day
Time to complete the job = 6 days

3. Answer: A) 12 seconds

Total length to cover = Sum of lengths of both trains = 250 + 250 = 500 meters
Relative speed = Sum of speeds of both trains = 60 + 90 = 150 km/h = 150 × (5/18) = 41.67 m/s
Time taken = Distance / Speed = 500 / 41.67 = 12 seconds

4. Answer: A) 24 hours

Rate of filling by pipe = 1/6 tank per hour
Rate of leakage = (1/6 - 1/8) = (4-3)/24 = 1/24 tank per hour
Time taken to empty the full tank = 1 ÷ (1/24) = 24 hours

5. Answer: A) 12 km/h

Let the distance be d km.
Time taken for onward journey = d/10 hours
Time taken for return journey = d/15 hours
Total time = d/10 + d/15 = (3d + 2d)/30 = 5d/30 = d/6 hours
Total distance = 2d km
Average speed = Total distance / Total time = 2d / (d/6) = 12 km/h

6. Answer: A) 10 days

Work done by 1 man in 1 day = 1/(5 × 37) = 1/185
Work done by 1 woman in 1 day = 1/(7 × 37) = 1/259
Work done by 7 men in 1 day = 7 × 1/185 = 7/185
Work done by 6 women in 1 day = 6 × 1/259 = 6/259
Work done by 7 men and 6 women in 1 day = 7/185 + 6/259 ≈ 1/10
Time taken to complete the work = 10 days
